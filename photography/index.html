Photography

I’ve been fascinated with photography since I was a kid.*

Luckily for me, my high school had a TV Studio extracurricular that gave me the opportunity to spend a lot of time using DSLRs, shoulder-mount video cameras and a few different professional photo and video editing softwares, so I was able to get a reasonable amount of practice fairly early on.

Now I mainly use a DJI Mavic Air 2, a Sony Cybershot that is a few years old but still great, and I have a Rylo (r.i.p.) that I take out every now an then.

Here are some of the coolest/my favorite photos I’ve taken over the last few years. They’re not all amazing or high-effort photos, but I like them for one reason or another, maybe you will too.
